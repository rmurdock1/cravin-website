Our Story

Rooted in tradition, served with love.

Cravin began with a simple idea: bring the authentic taste of Jamaica to New York neighborhoods. What started as one kitchen has grown into three beloved locations — each one built on family recipes, fresh ingredients, and a deep love for our culture.

Every plate we serve carries the warmth and spirit of the island.
